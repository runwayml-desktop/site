About Ruwnay

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Ruwnay emerged from a vision to revolutionize motion design education by bridging the gap between traditional design principles and cutting-edge AI technology. Our journey began when a group of industry veterans recognized the growing need for comprehensive education in AI-assisted motion design.

Our Mission

At Ruwnay, we're committed to empowering creators with the knowledge and tools they need to thrive in the evolving landscape of motion design. We believe in making advanced technology accessible while maintaining the artistic integrity of design.

What Sets Us Apart

Industry-Leading Expertise

Our instructors bring decades of combined experience from leading studios and technology companies, ensuring our curriculum reflects real-world industry demands.

Innovative Learning Approach

We combine traditional motion design principles with cutting-edge AI tools, creating a unique learning experience that prepares students for the future of creative work.

Community-Driven Growth

Our vibrant community of learners and professionals creates an environment where knowledge sharing and collaboration thrive.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Constantly exploring new technologies and methodologies in motion design
  • Quality: Maintaining high standards in education and student support
  • Accessibility: Making advanced education available to learners worldwide
  • Community: Fostering a supportive environment for creative growth

Our Team

Ruwnay is powered by a diverse team of motion designers, AI specialists, and education experts. Our leadership team brings together expertise from major tech companies, creative agencies, and educational institutions.

Global Reach

While headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts, our digital platform reaches students and professionals across the globe. We're proud to have alumni from over 50 countries, creating a truly international community of motion design innovators.

Industry Partnerships

We collaborate with leading software companies, design studios, and AI technology providers to ensure our students have access to the latest tools and industry opportunities.

Environmental Commitment

As a digital-first education platform, we're committed to minimizing our environmental impact. We implement sustainable practices in our operations and encourage eco-friendly approaches in motion design work.
